Our Business | History

Chill & Eat iKota was born out of the desire to challenge what is the status quo. Founded in November of 2017 2Selai started as a small shop in the garage of a Soweto home. Chill & Eat iKota has an events arm with which we can reach a wider range of cliental and explore our culinary expertise in the Kota industry. Our primary product offering is a Kota, a hollowed-out quarter loaf filled with a variety of ingredients.
